📖 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

🔍 How do I verify evidence on the blockchain?

Go to the 'Evidence Verification' page and enter the hash to check its authenticity.

📂 How do I upload a new evidence file?

Click 'Add File' in the repository page and follow the upload process.

🔑 What to do if I forget my login credentials?

Contact the system administrator or use the password recovery option on the login page.
